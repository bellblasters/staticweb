Kiran who wasnt 100% fit joined Vinod. At the end of 10th over, Bell Blaster required run a ball and they were going at the required rate before Vinod teared apart Sridhar for 29 runs in 15th over. The match was done and dusted and Bell Blasters won the match in 17th over with 7 wickets in hand. Vinod was adjudged man of the match for his aggressive unbeaten knock of 82 runs in 48 balls (7 fours and 5 sixes).
