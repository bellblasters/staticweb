AMR Group 2019 CTCL T16

Round1 :

Bell Blasters vs Sunrisers

- Denver, CO

- Mar 24th, 2019

Toss: Sunrisers (Batting)

Brief Score:
Sunrisers Batting: 74/9 in 16.0 overs. Nikhil Kunche 21 (26), Siva Pillalamarri 21 (29)
Bell Blasters Bowling: Gautam Trivedi 4-0-22-4, Ravi Teja Nekkanti 3-0-12-2
Bell Blasters Batting: 78/4 in 12.4 overs. Shankar U 18 (24), Tamim Alokozay 27 (27)
Sunrisers Bowling: Abhishek V 3.4-0-17-2

Man of the Match: Gautam Trivedi

Full Scorecard: Click Here

Brief Summary:
Bell Blasters - The Colorado Cricket Enthusiasts played their 1st match of CTCL 2019 - T16 at Denver on last Sunday against Sunrisers. These teams faced each other in the past twice and Bell Blasters were victorious at both occasions. Bell Blasters lost their few key players from last season, but they added some young blood in the squad and they were ready to go for a kill against Sunrisers. Sunrisers won the toss and decided to bat first. Their openers had no answer to Bell Blasters' opening bowlers - Tamim and Ravi Teja. Rav Teja and Tamim grabbed 3 quick wickets and Sunrisers were struggling at 14/3 after mandatory power play of 4 overs. Bell Blasters' veteran fast bowler Gautam Trivedi came to the attack and picked up 3 wickets in single over and Sunrisers were 15/6. After that Sunrisers were playing for survival and Nikhil and Shiva were showing some resistance to reach a respectable total. Finally Sunrisers managed to score 74/9 in 16 overs. Gautam grabbed 4 wickets, Ravi Teja 2 wickets and 1 each for Tamim and Sathpathy. In reply Blasters started their run chase with a bang when Shailendra Ambani hooked one for 4 in the first over. But soon after that he went for an expensive drive out side the off and only managed to give a simple dolly to Sunrisers wicket keeper 4 (3). It was very windy and Sunriser's bowlers were looking for couple of wickets. But sensible batting from Shankar U 18 of 24 balls (1X6, 1X4)  and Ravi Teja 6 of 15 (0X4, 0X6) prevented Blasters to do any further damage. Unfortunately Ravi Teja was mankaded by Shiva and a poor decision from umpire sent Shankar home judging him caught behind. There was a clear day light between ball and bat. Later Bhushan 2 (3) came to crease and went for an expensive slog to throw his wicket. Bell Blasters were 4 down and couple of wickets at that time could turn the table. But Sunrisers lacked better bowling options in middle overs and Tamim scored a run a ball 27* (2X4, 1X6) to chase the score comfortably. Deba was not out at 4*(5). Gautam Trivedi was judged MoM for his amazing spell of swing bowling (4-0-22-4). Special mention: Shankar U (wk) took 5 catches.

Full Scorecard: Click Here

Brief Summary:
Bell Blasters - The Colorado Cricket Enthusiasts played their 1st match of CTCL 2018 T20 at Denver on Jun 10th against Falcons. Blasters won the toss and decided to field first. Blasters' fast bowling maestro Gautam Trivedi picked up a couple of wickets in power play and Falcons never got their momentum going. They finally managed to score 116/10 in 18.1 overs courtesy to Blasters' superb controlled bowling and excellent fielding. Rajesh A finished with 3 wickets, Gautam and Bhushan Hatwalne picked up 2 wickets each. Debapriyo, Vinayak Patil and Sridhar Vana picked 1 wicket each. While chasing Blasters' never got their momentum and lost 6 wickets for 39 in 8.4 overs. Debapriyo Kolay came out to bat and he continued to bat till 19th over scoring 41 out of only 31 balls (2X4, 2X6) before ran himself out. Deba and Gautam had a 50 runs partnership in only 7.1 overs. Gautam played a crucial role by scoring 16 (22). Blasters' were 9 wickets down and they had to score 4 runs in last over. Rajesh A and Rajesh Gudaru made sure to cross the winning lines with 2 balls to spare. Debapriyo Kolay was awarded Man of the match.

Brief Summary:
Bell Blasters - The Colorado Cricket Enthusiasts played their 2nd match of CTCL 2018 - T20 at Erie on Jun 17th against Hyderabad Blues. Blues won the toss and decided to bat first. The weather was overcast and drizzled at times. Blasters' fast bowlers took full advantage of the condition and half of the Blues batsmen went back to pavilion in first 10 overs. They were struggling for 39/5 after 10 overs. Sudhakar came to rescue and scored 52 of 40 balls and Blues finished their innings by scoring 117/10 in 19.1 overs. Gautam Trivedi, Bhushan Hatwalne and Debapriyo Kolay picked up 2 wickets each. While chasing Blasters lost their early wickets and they were 13/3 in 2 overs. Shankar and Ashish had 87 runs partnership in 12.5 overs. Shankar scored 38 of 36 balls (2X4, 2X6) and Ashish finished with unbeaten 54 of 50 balls (4X4, 1X6). Finally Bell Blasters won the match by 6 wickets with 2.5 overs to spare. Ashish Kumar was awarded Man of the Match for his match winning unbeaten half a ton
